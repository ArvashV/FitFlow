Fitness Tips

Workout Tips

  • - Start with 10-15 minutes of warm-up before intense exercises
  • - Focus on form over speed - quality beats quantity every time
  • - Rest days are just as important as workout days for muscle recovery
  • - Stay consistent - small daily efforts lead to big results

Nutrition Tips

  • - Eat protein within 30 minutes after your workout
  • - Drink water throughout the day, not just when you're thirsty
  • - Fill half your plate with vegetables for balanced nutrition
  • - Plan your meals ahead to avoid unhealthy food choices
